Asia Pacific University Blockchain & Cryptocurrency Club

Asia Pacific University Blockchain & Cryptocurrency Club (APUBCC), a university-led initiative, was founded on 12 August 2021 that aims to spread awareness about blockchain via social medias, discussions, research, gamification, seminars, seminars, conferences, etc. With the ultimate goal of promoting technology learning, as well as helping students and society to overcome common misconceptions.
